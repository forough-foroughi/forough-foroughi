# Metrics vs Logs

| Feature           | Metrics                           | Logs                              |
|-------------------|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Nature            | Numeric and statistical data       | Text-based and event-driven data   |
| Speed             | Very high (real-time)              | Delayed (batch processing)          |
| Accuracy / Detail | Low (high-level overview)          | Very high (fine-grained details)    |
| Primary Use Case  | Monitoring and alerting            | Troubleshooting and reporting       |
